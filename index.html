About MIND

MIcrosoft News Dataset (MIND) is a large-scale dataset for news recommendation, which consists of real user click logs and displayed news on the Microsoft News website. Given the historical news click data of each user, a news recommender system needs to provide personalized ranking of candidate news. The mission of MIND is serving as a good testbed for news recommendation methods and facilitating the research on recommender systems.

MIND contains 1,000,000 users, 161,013 English news articles and 15,777,377 news impression logs. Every news contains rich textual content including title, abstract, body, category and entities. Each impression log contains the click events, non-clicked events and historical news click behaviors of a user. On the MIND dataset, a well-performed news recommender system needs to accurately model news content and user interest simultaneously.
